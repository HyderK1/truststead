Get in Touch

If you would like to discuss a potential referral, block booking, partnership or property, please get in touch using the details below or the enquiry form.

We welcome contact from local authority commissioning teams, probation and criminal justice services, charities, voluntary and community groups, and property owners interested in supported housing partnerships.

For referral / placement enquiries, it helps if you can include:
  • Whether this is a spot placement, emergency or block booking enquiry
  • Age range, support needs and any known risks
  • Current accommodation / setting and desired move-on timeframe
  • Which local authority or organisation you are contacting from

We aim to respond to all enquiries as quickly as possible and can arrange a call to discuss specific needs in more detail.
