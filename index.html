Welcome to TrustStead

TrustStead Supported Living provides small, well-managed homes for people who are able to live semi-independently but benefit from structure, clear expectations and housing-related support. Our properties are located in residential areas and are designed to feel like real homes, not institutions.

Led by experienced professionals with backgrounds in supported housing, education, mentoring, youth work and central government, we understand both the day-to-day realities of residents and the responsibilities of commissioners and partner agencies.

We work alongside local authorities, probation, charities and community organisations to provide safe, stable accommodation that supports progression into training, work and longer-term independence.

Our Supported Living Services

TrustStead provides supported accommodation for people who can live semi-independently with the right level of structure and guidance. Our focus is on safe homes, clear expectations and practical support that helps residents stabilise and move forward.

We work with local authorities, probation and partner organisations to deliver flexible placements that are responsive to individual need while remaining consistent, transparent and manageable for commissioners.
